More about this app

Projects:

When you choose two regions above and press 'GO' objects from those regions are randomly chosen from the Metropolitan Museum of Art's sculpture collection.

Images and information about those objects, questions to ask yourself about the artworks, and a project idea will follow.

Recipes from the chosen region are available at the bottom of the project page.

You can refresh the project ideas if they do not suit the objects.

If you want new objects, come back to the homepage and choose regions again.

Flash cards:

Recently viewed objects will be stored as flash cards so you can quiz yourself on the object details later.

Clear the deck to remove all cards.
